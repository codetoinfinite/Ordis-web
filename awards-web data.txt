w# AWWWARDS WEB DATA - DEEP ANALYSIS
=========================================================
This master document contains the rigorous breakdown of components, animations, transitions, and user experience (UX) paradigms extracted from elite, award-winning websites (4wide, Infinite Field, Newmix, Tom Carder Media, Peachworlds, Thomas Mamfredas).

## 1. 4wide.jp (Creative Web Studio)
- **Aesthetic**: Premium Japanese minimalism, grid-based precision, hyper-smooth typography.
- **Components**:
  - Custom fluid cursor with magnetic snapping to actionable elements.
  - Asymmetric grid layouts with dynamic sticky sidebars.
- **Effects & Animations**:
  - **Preloader**: Typography-driven mask-reveals with a progress counter.
  - **Scroll**: Locomotive scroll (virtual scroll) causing ultra-smooth inertia.
  - **Transitions**: Page transitions use full-screen SVG paths (curved wipes) and delay DOM unmounting for fluid routing.
  - **Micro-interactions**: Hovering images applies a WebGL displacement filter (liquid distortion effect).

## 2. Infinite Field (HFT / Data-Driven)
- **Aesthetic**: High-tech, Brutalist-inspired dark mode, neon accents, data visualization.
- **Components**:
  - Interactive 3D WebGL particle system that acts as the "hero" background.
  - HUD-like typography panels floating above the 3D canvas.
- **Effects & Animations**:
  - **Scroll**: ScrollTrigger tied to the camera position of the Three.js scene (the camera flies through the particle field based on scroll progress).
  - **Interactions**: Mouse movement actively repels 3D particles (Raycaster in Three.js calculons distance to vertices).
  - **Typography**: Glitch-text reveals on scroll intersecting using SplitText.

## 3. NewMix Coffee
- **Aesthetic**: Vibrant, tactile, product-centric.
- **Components**:
  - 3D models of product packaging rendered directly in the DOM using react-three-fiber or plain Three.js.
  - Overlapping "card" sections that stack upon scrolling.
- **Effects & Animations**:
  - **Scroll**: Horizontal scroll sections triggered within a vertical scroll container (Pinning via GSAP).
  - **3D Interaction**: Product models rotate and scale based on scroll delta. When hovered, the models track the cursor slightly to give a sense of depth and weight.

## 4. Tom Carder Media (Cinematic Video)
- **Aesthetic**: Immersive, full-bleed media, cinematic pacing.
- **Components**:
  - Large-scale HTML5 `<video>` backgrounds masked by SVG typography or shapes.
- **Effects & Animations**:
  - **Hover Effects**: Hovering menu links dims the background and switches the background video instantaneously.
  - **Image Distortion**: Scrolling applies a dynamic skew and scale (momentum effect) to images based on the speed of the scroll.
  - **Transitions**: Soft fade-ins with staggered typography reveals (lines moving up from `overflow: hidden` wrappers).

## 5. Peachworlds (Autonomous Financial Consultant)
- **Aesthetic**: Monochromatic, high-contrast, philosophical tech.
- **Components**:
  - **The Centerpiece**: A continuously rotating, weathered 3D mineral stone (representing raw nature + AI logic).
  - Floating high-contrast typography cards that orbit or slide behind the 3D mineral.
- **Effects & Animations**:
  - **Scroll**: The 3D mineral dynamically shatters or reforms based on scroll depth, revealing inner glowing elements (AI core).
  - **Interactions**: Dragging the mouse rotates the environment lighting (HDRI) around the mineral, changing its shadows and reflections in real-time.
  - **Transitions**: Extreme precision in element placement using CSS Grid overlaying a fixed absolute Three.js canvas.

## 6. Thomas Mamfredas (Photography)
- **Aesthetic**: Editorial, raw, noise-textured grain over images.
- **Components**:
  - Custom WebGL sliders for image galleries.
- **Effects & Animations**:
  - **Image Reveal**: Images load with a curtain-pull effect or an RGB-split distortion shader.
  - **Cursor**: The cursor morphs into a "View" text or an arrow depending on the interactive zone.
  - **Parallax**: Subtle multi-layered parallax on high-resolution photography, preserving aspect ratios perfectly.

# UNIVERSAL SENIOR-DEV PATTERNS (AVOIDING "JUNIOR AI" VIBES)
1. **Separation of Layers**: 
   - A single `<canvas id="webgl-container">` fixed to the background `z-index: -1`.
   - HTML content scrolls naturally over it. Scroll values are passed to the WebGL render loop (no moving disjointed components).
2. **Performance (No Bulky Code)**:
   - RequestAnimationFrame (rAF) loops unified. Instead of 20 different `setInterval` or chaotic event listeners, there is ONE global `gsap.ticker` or custom rAF managing all scroll/mouse/3D updates.
   - Instanced meshes in Three.js (drawing thousands of objects with 1 draw call).
3. **Typography**:
   - `font-feature-settings` optimized. Clean, pixel-perfect alignment using `rem` padding/margins. NO random absolute positioning for layout text.
4. **Resilience**:
   - Resize events debounced. Camera aspect ratios update seamlessly.
